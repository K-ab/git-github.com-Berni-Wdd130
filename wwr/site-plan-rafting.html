Overview

Purpose

Your rafting will be directed by our inflatable boat. You will pass through passages with large volumes of water, rapids and many other small "obstacles" during your raft descent. The only prerequisite to enjoy the sensations of Rafting is to know how to swim at least 20 meters! More than putting on your swimsuit and starting this refreshing activity while exploring the "San Pedro" region, you also need to be in good general physical condition.

Audience

Happy Rafting Company will allow you to enjoy the lake in the same day and that the Dranse constitutes a river imprinted with vegetation and rocks to live an invigorating moment of relaxation in the middle of the spray. We invite you to embark on our boats that can accommodate between and or 8 people maximum for a remarkable descent. Let yourself be tempted and come live. this course will offer you a descent of great atmosphere alternating rapids and quieter portions. An activity to do with family or friends: spray, sensations and laughter guaranteed! Accessible to anyone who can swim and over 8 years old. You will be supervised by a state-certified instructor and all technical equipment will be provided to you.]
